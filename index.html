About our Institute

A career in beauty therapy is highly rewarding and the industry is constantly evolving, so you will continue to be challenged throughout your career.

who we are

ITHEMBA ELIHLE INSTITUTE is a private training education organization that offers skills development, a competitive range of learning programmes and training services to all. The aim of the institution is to offer career-focused Outcomes Based Education and Training (OBET), and to enhance the projects of employment and self-employment for its valuable learners.

Our learning programs offer both theory and practical to empower the students for the 21st Century Beauty Industry. We are committed to creating a strong and continuous improvement in human performance. Following that we customize our programmes which are specifically designed to build, sustain the skills and behaviours that are essential to your success.
