Do you Enjoy making people feel good about themselves: You’re in the right Place!
Students will learn to apply their newly developed skills with the learning environment that is challenging and demands active participation. Our aim to educate and inspire an individual for a career success.
At Ithemba Elihle Institute we offer quality full-time, short courses and part qualification programmes.
Certificate in Beauty and Nail technology NQF 4[80646].
We also offer courses and classes on a part-time basis, giving you the opportunity to combine study and career development with your working life.
Is Short Courses and Part Time for you?

If life and your career is a full-time job and you can’t study full-time but you need to learn more to earn more or advance yourself. If so, then the Short Course and Part- Qualification Courses are definitely for you!

We understand that professionals and more mature adults need their studies to fit in with their lifestyle or career.
Lectures are held on selected week days or evenings (17:30-20:30), with certain courses requiring attendance on Saturday mornings (09:00-12:00) on special request.
• All our courses are offered in English.

Ithemba Elihle Institute offers you:
• Top lecturers - providing you with a top education;
• Professional respect;
• Multiple teaching methods - which provide a variety of study stimuli;
• An extended study period that is flexible and optimises learning;
• Smaller classes with peer interaction.
• Nationally Approved and Accredited SAQA Unit Standards through Services Seta's part qualifications.

REQUIREMENTS
ACADEMIC REQUIREMENTS

• Latest results obtained | highest qualification (Level3) Grade 11/12
• Copy of ID/Passport
• Copy of ID/Passport of person responsible for payment

Entry REQUIREMENTS

• Minimum age 16 and older
• NQF level 3 (Grade 11/12)
• Communication and literacy
• Numeracy and mathematical skills at Level 3
[Recognition of prior learning] is also provided for learners with past experience or alternatively learners with other qualifications applicable, will also be considered.



ADMISSIONS PROCESS

Registration documentation completed and signed

Step one

Registration application will be reviewed and verified.

Step Two

Learner will be notified and registered once the deposit amount has been received.

Step Three

Learner will be loaded on to the system and a student number will be generated.

Step Four

Contact will be made to ensure that the learner knows the scheduled times and class time tables.

COSTS AND PRICE STRUCTURES

The various course modules or unit standards will carry a school fee which will include a kit, learning material and uniform.
A required deposit to be paid on or before the commencement of the training.
The balance of the course fee is payable in equal instalments.
